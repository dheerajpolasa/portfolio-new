THE PERSON

Backend engineer by trade, curious human by nature. I have built systems that handle millions of users at companies like Amazon and PayPal — but the laptop closing is where the real arc begins.

I relate deeply to Naruto Uzumaki — the grind, the growth, the absolute refusal to give up no matter how brutal the arc gets. Believe it.

Attack on Titan hit different. The moral complexity, the stakes, the storytelling. That is how I want to approach engineering — deeply, with intention and weight.

I love picking up things that have nothing to do with my job — manga drawing, perfumery, the psychology of sneaker drops, cars. The world is massive and opportunities are endless.

Currently watching the Vitrus GT facelift announcements like a hawk. Open to interesting convos, collabs, and rabbit holes.
